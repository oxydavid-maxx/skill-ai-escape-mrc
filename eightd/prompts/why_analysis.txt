Produce a chain of >=10 Whys for the assigned quadrant.

Quadrants:
- q1_trc_nc: Technical root cause, Non-Conformance (direct technical
  cause that produced the defect)
- q2_trc_nd: Technical root cause, Non-Detection (detection method that
  failed to catch it)
- q3_mrc_nc: Managerial root cause, Non-Conformance (process/governance
  gap that allowed it)
- q4_mrc_nd: Managerial root cause, Non-Detection (control system gap
  allowing detection miss)

Each Why MUST be a genuinely new causal insight, not a rephrase of the
prior why. MRC quadrants (q3, q4) must land at management-system level
(process, governance, policy) NOT code-level ("delete function",
"add check"). Stop at the deepest controllable cause.
